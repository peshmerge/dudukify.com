About Dudukify

The fact you are reading this means you are either a Duduk player or you know what Duduk is. Therefore, I don't have to tell you how old is the Duduk or how beautiful, heartwarming and melancholic it's! Anyway if you haven't heard of Duduk, I kindly suggest you to read this excellent and short piece Duduk - Music of Armenia

My name is Peshmerge Morad, a Kurdish-Syrian-Dutch software developer and a Duduk player based in the Netherlands.
I have built this small and handy website (it's a PWA :D ) to:

  • tune my Duduk before playing since my ear isn't musical at all ¯\_(ツ)_/¯ ;
  • have Duduk dam tones in the background while playing Duduk.
